Frequently Asked Questions

We answer common questions about our development process, tech stack, and what it is like to work with our engineering team.

What technologies and frameworks do you work with?

We work across the modern tech stack including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Go, and TypeScript on the application side, with PostgreSQL, Redis, and MongoDB for data. For infrastructure we use AWS, GCP, Docker, and Kubernetes. We choose the right tools for each project based on your requirements.

How do you handle project scope and changes during development?

We use an agile methodology with two-week sprints and regular check-ins. Scope changes are a natural part of building software -- we manage them through a transparent backlog, clear prioritization, and honest communication about trade-offs so nothing derails your timeline.

Will I own the code and intellectual property?

Absolutely. You own 100% of the code, designs, and intellectual property we create for you. We deliver everything in a well-organized repository with full documentation, ensuring your team or future developers can maintain and extend the codebase independently.

How do you ensure code quality and reliability?

Every piece of code goes through peer review, automated testing (unit, integration, and end-to-end), and CI/CD pipelines. We maintain high test coverage, use static analysis tools, and follow industry best practices for security and performance from the start.

How do I get started with Forge?

Getting started is simple. Book a free discovery call and we will discuss your project goals, technical requirements, and timeline. From there, we provide a detailed proposal and roadmap so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.
